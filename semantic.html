MRINARA PASTA::

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt.Boil the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Heat a little bit of olive oil on medium heat , add garlic and onion. Add chopped tomatos or canned crushed tomatos. After 1-2 mins season with salt, pepper, oregano and basil. You can also add sugar if you want.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce. Toss well, you can add a splash of red wine for more flavour.
  4. Finish by adding grated Parmesan.
