ACOUSTIC GUITAR:

STRINGS:

Normally a guitar has 6 strings but it can also have 7,8 or even 12.

BODY:

The body is the main shape of the guitar.

HEADSTOCK:

Headstock is the top part of the neck where we find th tuning pegs.

HOW IS IT PLAYED:

A guitar can be played using chords(when a group of notes are played at the same time)
Picking is when we play a single string, it can be used in melodies or riffs.
In a song there can be used many chords.
